YOUNG AMBASSADORS FOR CHANGE


Mission Statement

To safe guard and enhance the young people towards radical changes in their spirit, moral, social and economical lives. To make a maximum utilization of every opportunity in order to bring changes in the lives of so many young people.


Aim & Objectives

  • To promote mental and physical changes in the lives of so many youths.
  • To involve youth in agriculture, poetry, music, sports and peace building in our broken society.
  • To value the contribution of every young people for the betterment of our modern society.
  • To build the capacity in the lives of the young people in terms of skills, knowledge and enterpreneurship.
  • To foster the creativity, radical thinking and active participation of the young people in the development and transformation of our society.
